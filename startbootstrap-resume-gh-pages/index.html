The Witch's Wood and the Wandering Wharf

name@email.com

The colonies are a dangerous place. Deep in the woods, ancient magics stir, bringing to life long dead covens of witches and warlocks. Along the shores, fog rolls in and people wander off or mutter unintelligible nonsense.

Despite this, people have come here to stay. Governors exert their will over the region. Too far away for any form of oversight, each colony is at the whim of their governor. The lives of the colonists range from free people to near slavery.

This is the reality you live in. Here you've come to make a new life for yourself. You must use all of your wits, strength, and cunning to survive in a land that is determined to rid itself of any outsiders.

About

    I'm Squid. I'm a Programmer turned Marine Biologist and an avid player of Dungeons and Dragons. I've been a Dungeon Master for years now and this is a passion prject for me. I've always loved stories and running RPGs is one of my favorite things. Some of my favorite stories are those by Edgar Allen Poe and H.P. Lovecraft and they're both pretty big influences on me. In my spare time I love being outside in nature, taking hikes, camping, and fishing. Otherwise, stories of all sorts have been my passion, with reading, watching movies, and especially playing video games being big to me. I'm very excited to set out on this project and bring my passion as a Dungeon Master to the world.
